Supplementary lessons -
Your chance to stay fit for the German school

Not all countries allow children to be homeschooled. Compulsory schooling requires that lessons be taught at a local school. Nevertheless, many emigrants want to give their children the opportunity to attend school in German. Additional instruction in the foreign languages English, French and Spanish is often requested.
For this purpose we have developed an independent hybrid concept, so that female students can take advantage of additional offers in German outside of normal school hours.
Each subject can be booked semi-annually as an independent module.

Dynamic Schedule

Hybrid Model

While in the full-time school we follow the official guidelines of the German KMK, we can work more dynamically in the supplementary model. Here, too, we teach the central contents of the respective class levels (from the pool of the KMK guidelines), but we set priorities. In this way, we can intensify central content and dispense with marginal detail knowledge. This helps to balance out the amount of material and gives us and the students flexibility. Additional didactic models such as the "Flipped Classroom Model" use additional capacity, saving teaching time and increasing learning efficiency.
This allows learners to work intensively in the German target language, but can schedule their time more flexibly.
All lessons take place in our virtual classroom online with a regular teacher.

Lessons

60 minutes

Since the hybrid model allows us to relax the fullness of hours and make preparation more flexible, we use the 60-minute model for the supplementary hours. This is more relaxed for students who already have lessons at another school.
Classes are held at times which in most countries correspond to early afternoon to evening. The weekend is also included. 

Subjects

Each subject can be booked as a semi-annual course (6 months with vacations). From 8 students on, a course is formed. Please contact us directly so that we can explain our concepts and discuss possible teaching times.
